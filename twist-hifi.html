The convenient, safe and efficient way to take care of your succulents.

LOG IN

The user can log in their own account and enjoy the exclusive service for their plant.

INFORMATION OF PLANT

After identified the breed of the succulent, the app will show the information of the succulent.

MAKE A PLAN

According to the information of the succulent, the app will  automatically  make a growing plan for the succulent.

MONITOR THE CONDITION

The app can show the real time condition of the succulent, the data is come from the TWIST smart pot.

HEALTH REPORT

The health condition will be recorded. After analysis, the user will get a accurate health report.

SIDE BAR

The app not only can  supervise the succulent, but also can provide multiple services to the user, including on-line shopping, social, reading.

WARNING

When the problem occurs, the app will notify the user timly.

SCAN THE PLANT

The app can identify the breed of the succulent through the camera.
